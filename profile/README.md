# Basilisk Robotics

Basilisk Robotics is building robotic systems for underwater inspection, cleaning, and maintenance.

We are focused on making marine infrastructure and vessel maintenance safer, faster, and more scalable through practical robotics.

## What we are working on

- Underwater robotic platforms
- Hull inspection and maintenance workflows
- Marine robotics hardware and software
- Field-deployable systems for real-world environments

Most of our engineering repositories are private while we are actively developing the platform.
